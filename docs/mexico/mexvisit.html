What is the MexVisit® Mexico Auto Insurance supplement?

MexVisit® is Mexpro.com's exclusive branded Travel Assistance product, and is not available from any other agent or underwriter. When our optional MexVisit® Travel Assistance coverage is purchased, in addition to the regular tourist auto insurance policy, coverage includes not only excellent auto insurance protection from Mexico's finest underwriters, but also the best travel assistance coverage available in the marketplace. Coverage is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, through a live case monitoring contact center. MexVisit® is available as an option on all policies.
