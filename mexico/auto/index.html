Why you need special Mexico Insurance

Liability

Liability car insurance covers damages to another person or other property resulting from an accident you cause. Liability only policies are the most basic auto insurance coverage, and the minimum required to have when driving in Mexico. There are no coverages to your vehicle with this policy.

Full coverage

Full coverage refers to liability coverages plus coverage for your vehicle. Our Premium policy has increased limits, low deductibles, and additional coverages which make it among the broadest policies available in Mexico.

Temporary vehicle permits

If you plan to drive a vehicle beyond the Mexico "Free Zone" you will need to purchase a Mexico Vehicle Import Permit. The temporary vehicle import permit can be obtained from Banjercito, the official Mexican issuing agency, and may be purchased online at the Banjercito website or at the Mexican border.
