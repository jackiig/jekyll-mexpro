More About ABA

ABA Seguros is one of Mexico's finest insurance companies. It provides property and casualty products that include auto, home and business insurance. ABA was founded in 1956 in Monterrey, Mexico, and has grown to a nationally recognized company working with 2,000 agents in Mexico, as well as many auto dealerships, banks, and direct channels.

Owned by ACE Seguros, ABA is a part of one of the world's largest insurance companies ACE INA Holdings, Inc. A Member of the Ace Group of companies, which is an A+ A.M. Best rated insurance company, ABA's financial condition ensures they have the ability to pay their claims. ABA was purchased by ACE because of their personal lines insurance presence, as ABA is the fourth largest auto insurance company in Mexico. Because of their size, they have a superior claims infrastructure, which ensure Mexpro's clients are taken care of in a quick and professional manner. Mexpro has worked with ABA Seguros since 2006, as their exclusive distributor of Mexico Tourist Auto insurance. This partnership has allowed Mexpro to grow significantly with ABA Seguros in the past several years, and has allowed Mexpro to create programs and products that are unique to ABA such as their handling of Medical Payments, Legal Assistance, and Travel Assistance.
