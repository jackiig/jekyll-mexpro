How to Cancel a Mexico Auto Insurance Policy

Cancel a PolicyÂ Not Yet in Effect

All Mexpro cancellations must be sent to us in writing; you can complete our quick online cancellation form or you can log in to your account to cancel. Policies cancelled before they are in effect, before the date the insurance coverage begins, will receive a full refund. See our page on in effect cancelations if your coverage has begun.

All refunds will be credited back to the card that was originally used. Depending on your credit card company or bank, you should see the refund within a week.

Please note, a phone call will not cancel a policy.
