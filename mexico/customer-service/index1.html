Refunds

Mexico Tourist Auto

If you cancel a policy rior to it being in use, you will receive a full refund. If the policy is in effect, daily policies, six month policies over 20 days, annual policies over 30 days, and the policy fees are not refundable. Six-month and annual policies are discounted and will be prorated per the terms and conditions of the policy.

Mexico Homeowners

Prior to the policy being in use, you will receive a full refund. If the policy is in effect, cancellations are prorated per the terms and conditions of the policy.

Mexico Tourist Auto Claims

1
Report the Claim

[IN MEXICO]

At the scene of the accident, prior to leaving, call the toll free number listed on your policy. The claims reporting toll free number will only work from a Mexico land line or a cell phone with a Mexico calling plan.

2
Follow the Mexico Insurance
Company's Instructions

[BEFORE YOU LEAVE MEXICO]

When the adjuster arrives, have your policy, registrations, and drivers license available. Keep a copy of the adjusters accident report and any police reports.

3
Follow up

[ONCE BACK IN THE U.S./CANADA]

Call the Mexico Insurance Company's U.S./Canada toll free number and have your claims and policy number available.

Mexico Homeowners Claims
