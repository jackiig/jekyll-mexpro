Debris Removal

The cost of the cost of cleaning up your property after a covered loss.

Debris Removal Coverage

Debris removal refers to the cost of cleaning up your property after a covered loss. This includes the expense of demolition, clean-up and disposal.

Maximum/Minimum Limits

The minimum limit of coverage for debris removal is $5000 USD. The maximum limit is $100,000 USD.

Deductibles

Debris removal coverage is not subject to a deductible.
