Refunds

Mexico Tourist Auto

If you cancel a policy rior to it being in use, you will receive a full refund. If the policy is in effect, daily policies, six month policies over 20 days, annual policies over 30 days, and the policy fees are not refundable. Six-month and annual policies are discounted and will be prorated per the terms and conditions of the policy.

Mexico Homeowners

Prior to the policy being in use, you will receive a full refund. If the policy is in effect, cancellations are prorated per the terms and conditions of the policy.
